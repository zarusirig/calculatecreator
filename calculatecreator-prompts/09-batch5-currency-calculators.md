### How We Calculate (175 words)
**Diamond to USD Conversion:**
- **Official Rate:** 1 Diamond ≈ $0.005 USD (200 diamonds = $1)
- **TikTok's Cut:** TikTok keeps approximately 50% of gift value
- **What You Receive:** Roughly half of what viewers spend

**The Full Flow:**
1. Viewer buys TikTok Coins ($1 = ~100 coins, varies by purchase size)
2. Viewer sends gift during LIVE (gifts cost different coin amounts)
3. Creator receives Diamonds (approximately 50% of coin value)
4. Creator converts Diamonds to USD ($0.005 per diamond)
5. Creator withdraws to PayPal or bank (minimum $100)

**Why the 50% Cut?**
TikTok claims operational costs, app store fees, and platform maintenance. This is standard across most live streaming platforms (Twitch, YouTube take similar cuts).

**Regional Variations:**
Conversion rates may vary slightly by country. US and UK creators typically see the standard rate.
