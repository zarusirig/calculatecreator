## Calculator 1: Follower Growth Calculator (/calculators/follower-growth)
Current: ~180 words. Target: 850+ words.

### Introduction (75 words)
Project your future follower count based on current growth rate. Understanding your growth trajectory helps set realistic milestones, plan content strategy, and track progress toward monetization thresholds (10K for Creator Fund, 1K for LIVE). Sustainable growth (2-5% daily) beats viral spikes for long-term success.

### How We Calculate (175 words)
**Formula:** Future Followers = Current Followers × (1 + Daily Growth Rate)^Days

**Compound Growth Explained:**
- Growth compounds—small daily gains accumulate significantly
- 1% daily growth = 37x increase over one year
- 0.5% daily growth = 6x increase over one year

**Finding Your Growth Rate:**
1. Go to TikTok Analytics > Followers
2. Compare follower count 7 or 30 days apart
3. Calculate: (New - Old) / Old / Days × 100

**Limitations:**
- Assumes consistent growth (reality varies)
- Viral videos create spikes not captured
- Growth typically slows as accounts get larger

### Benchmarks (125 words)
| Growth Rate | Rating | Typical For |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| 5%+ daily | Viral Growth | Trending creators, hard to sustain |
| 2-5% daily | Strong Growth | Active creators with viral potential |
| 0.5-2% daily | Steady Growth | Healthy, sustainable trajectory |
| 0.1-0.5% daily | Slow Growth | Established accounts or inconsistent posting |
| <0.1% daily | Stagnant | Content or strategy needs refresh |

**Note:** Growth rate naturally decreases as follower count increases. A 1% daily rate is exceptional at 100K followers but expected at 1K.

### Why It Matters (150 words)
**📊 Goal Setting**
Knowing your trajectory helps set achievable milestones. If you're growing at 1% daily from 5K followers, you'll hit 10K (Creator Fund eligibility) in ~70 days.

**📈 Strategy Validation**
Track growth rate before and after strategy changes. If a new content type increases growth rate, you've found what works.

**💰 Monetization Planning**
Plan when you'll hit monetization thresholds:
- 1,000 followers: TikTok LIVE eligibility
- 10,000 followers: Creator Fund eligibility
- 100,000 followers: Premium brand deal territory

Use our [TikTok Money Calculator](/calculators/tiktok-money) to project earnings at future follower counts.

### How to Accelerate Growth (200 words)
**8 Proven Growth Strategies:**

1. **Post consistently** - 1-3 times daily maintains momentum
2. **Optimize first 3 seconds** - Hook viewers immediately
3. **Ride trends early** - Early adopters get more distribution
4. **Engage with your niche** - Comment on similar creators' content
5. **Cross-promote** - Share TikToks on Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
6. **Create series content** - Gives viewers reason to follow for more
7. **Collaborate** - Duets and stitches expose you to new audiences
8. **Analyze what works** - Double down on your highest-performing formats

**What Slows Growth:**
- Posting inconsistently (algorithm penalizes gaps)
- Chasing trends outside your niche (confuses algorithm)
- Ignoring analytics (missing optimization opportunities)
- Buying followers (destroys organic reach)
