## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

tiktokcalculator.net has **350+ content nodes** (180 app pages + 257 MDX articles) covering TikTok creator monetization — a strong content foundation. However, **7 critical systemic failures** are actively preventing the site from ranking. The site scores **42/1000 on Topical Authority** (Vastness 7 × Depth 3 × Momentum 2).

The ranking failure is not a content volume problem. It is a **structural, linking, consistency, and trust signal problem** compounded by a domain migration that likely severed historical authority.

| # | Root Cause | Impact | Effort to Fix |
|---|-----------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | **Domain migration without redirect** — calculatecreator.com likely not 301-redirecting to tiktokcalculator.net | Critical | 1 day |

| 3 | **28 cannibalization groups** — ~45 pages compete for the same queries (brand deals: 14 pages, creator fund: 15, engagement rate: 14) | Critical | 2-3 weeks |

| 5 | **19 factual contradictions** across pages — Creator Rewards RPM off by 10-25x, gift values wrong, follower tier definitions inconsistent | High | 1 week |

### 4.2 Schema Markup Strategy (Skill Output: 06-schema-markup-strategy.md)

**3 Critical Schema Errors:**
1. `ComparisonTable` — not a valid schema.org type (will fail validation)
2. Logo URL in `hub-page-schema.ts` references `/logo.png` instead of `/images/tt-calculator-logo.png`
3. `FinancialProduct` used incorrectly for RPM data

**5 High-Priority Gaps:**
1. HowTo schema exists but deployed on **zero pages** — missing rich results on 50+ guides
2. No entity linking (`about`/`mentions` with Wikidata `sameAs`) on any Article schema
3. Organization `sameAs` array is empty — no Knowledge Graph anchoring
4. Duplicate Organization/WebSite schema across 3 files with conflicting descriptions
5. Calculator type inconsistency — mix of `WebApplication` and `SoftwareApplication`

**What's working:** BreadcrumbList, FAQPage on 71 pages, CollectionPage+ItemList on hubs, Dataset schema on data pages

## PART 5: SERP & AUDIENCE ANALYSIS

### 5.1 SERP Analysis (Skill Output: 08-serp-analysis.md)

**Zero pages are currently snippet-eligible.** Five systemic issues:

1. **No answer-first paragraphs** — every page opens with 50-80 word descriptive text instead of 25-40 word direct answers
2. **Data rendered as styled divs, not HTML `<table>`** — Google cannot extract table snippets
3. **FAQ answers too long (50-80 words)** — PAA needs 15-30 words, answer-first
4. **FAQs hidden in `<details>` accordions** — deprioritized for snippet extraction
5. **No comparison tables** on comparison-oriented pages

**Impact of fixing:** All 8 top query pages would become snippet-eligible. Estimated time to SERP feature capture: 4-8 weeks after implementation.

### 5.2 Audience Journey Mapping (Skill Output: 08-audience-journey-mapping.md)

**4 Audience Segments Identified:**
1. **Aspiring Creator (25%)** — pre-monetization, 0-5K followers, curiosity-driven
2. **Growing Creator (40%)** — 5K-100K followers, actively monetizing, largest/most valuable
3. **Established Creator (20%)** — 100K+ followers, business operators seeking optimization
4. **Brand/Agency Professional (15%)** — marketing managers, talent evaluators, highest per-visit value

**12 Journey Paths Designed** with entry points and 3-5 page sequences.

**7 Content Gaps by Audience:**
1. Segment A (Aspiring) — thin "what is" and "how does" entry-level content
2. Segment D (Brand/Agency) — almost entirely creator-framed, needs buyer-perspective guides
3. Cross-linking between evaluation-stage pages is weak
4. No retention mechanism (no newsletter, no "what changed" page)
5. International creators underserved beyond 12 country pages
6. Intermediate-to-advanced transition content missing
7. Pre-platform evaluation content absent

**Content Type Ratio:** Currently 58% informative / 24% commercial / 18% transactional. Target: 70/20/10 — informational base needs expansion.
