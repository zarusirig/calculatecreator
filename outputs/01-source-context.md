## 1. Brand Identity

- **Business:** TT Calculator is an independent calculator-and-editorial platform that helps TikTok creators estimate earnings, benchmark performance metrics, price brand deals, and plan monetization strategies. It publishes 35+ interactive calculators, 100+ long-form guides, niche-specific data pages, platform comparison studies, and a TikTok industry news section. The site is entirely free to use with no login required.
- **Monetization:** Programmatic display advertising (Google AdSense / Mediavine-tier), affiliate links to creator tools and services, and potential future sponsored data reports. There is no SaaS subscription, no paywall, and no direct product sales. Revenue scales with organic traffic volume and page depth per session.
- **Unique Perspective:** Numbers-first, assumption-transparent creator analytics. Every calculator shows its methodology, inputs, and limitations. The site does not make earnings promises; it provides directional models with benchmarks sourced from publicly available creator data, platform documentation, and industry reports. The editorial voice is practical and analytical, not aspirational or hype-driven.
- **Primary Audience:** TikTok creators at all stages -- from pre-monetization creators (under 1,000 followers) researching what is possible, to mid-tier creators (10K-500K followers) pricing brand deals and optimizing RPM, to established creators (500K+) comparing multi-platform income and planning tax strategy. Secondary audience: brands and agencies evaluating influencer rates and campaign ROI.

## 2. Central Entity

**TikTok Creator Monetization**

- **Definition:** The complete system by which TikTok creators convert content, audience, and platform features into income -- encompassing Creator Fund/Rewards payouts, brand deal sponsorships, LIVE gift earnings, TikTok Shop affiliate commissions, ad revenue, and all supporting metrics (engagement rate, RPM, watch time, viral potential) that determine earning capacity.
- **Scope:** Everything a TikTok creator needs to know to understand, estimate, optimize, and grow their income from TikTok. This includes:
  - Revenue streams: Creator Fund, Creator Rewards Program, brand deals, LIVE gifts (coins/diamonds), TikTok Shop, ad revenue, Series payouts
  - Performance metrics: engagement rate, RPM, CPM, watch time, completion rate, follower growth rate, viral potential score, save rate, share ratio
  - Business operations: tax planning, brand deal negotiation, rate cards, media kits, contract templates, legal compliance (FTC disclosure), LLC formation
  - Platform mechanics: algorithm behavior, For You page distribution, monetization eligibility requirements, payment schedules
  - Comparative context: TikTok vs YouTube vs Instagram earnings, cross-platform strategy
  - Data and benchmarks: earnings by country, RPM by niche, engagement rates by follower tier, brand deal rates by category
- **Boundaries -- What it does NOT include:**
  - General social media marketing strategy not specific to TikTok creator income
  - TikTok advertising from the brand/advertiser perspective (except where it directly affects creator earnings, e.g., Spark Ads for creators)
  - TikTok app development, API integration, or technical platform documentation
  - Personal entertainment content consumption (watching TikTok, trending dances, pop culture commentary)
  - Other platforms as primary subjects (YouTube, Instagram, Twitch are covered only in comparison to TikTok)
  - E-commerce strategy beyond TikTok Shop
  - Influencer marketing agency operations

## 3. Central Search Intent

**"Calculate, estimate, and optimize TikTok creator income"**

The unifying purpose across every page: users arrive wanting to quantify something about their TikTok monetization -- how much they can earn, whether their metrics are good, what rate to charge, how to improve their numbers, or what benchmarks to target.

- **Primary Predicates:** calculate, estimate, earn, convert, benchmark, compare, optimize, price, increase, check
- **Secondary Predicates:** understand, plan, track, improve, negotiate, qualify, maximize, project
- **User Goal:** Transform uncertainty about TikTok income into actionable numbers. Users want to go from "I don't know what I should earn / charge / expect" to "Now I have a specific number, benchmark, or strategy based on data."

## 7. Context Shifting Rules

When the Source Context encounters adjacent or overlapping topics, the processing angle and depth shift as follows:

### Entity Processing Through Source Context Lens

- **"TikTok algorithm"** --> Process through monetization lens: How does the algorithm affect RPM, earnings per view, and brand deal eligibility? NOT: How to game the algorithm for virality.
- **"Engagement rate"** --> Process as an earnings multiplier and brand deal qualification metric. NOT: A vanity metric or social proof signal.
- **"Follower count"** --> Process as a monetization milestone (1K = LIVE access, 10K = brand deal threshold, 100K = Creator Rewards eligibility). NOT: A popularity contest metric.
- **"TikTok Shop"** --> Process through creator affiliate/seller profit margins. NOT: Through consumer shopping experience or brand marketing strategy.
- **"Content creation"** --> Process through ROI lens: What content types generate the highest RPM, engagement, and brand deal value? NOT: Creative inspiration or artistic expression.
- **"Tax / Legal"** --> Process specifically for self-employed creator income from TikTok. NOT: General tax advice or corporate tax strategy.
- **"Country / Region"** --> Process through earnings differential: How does creator pay vary by geography? NOT: Cultural content trends or regional audience behavior.
- **"YouTube / Instagram"** --> Process as earnings comparison benchmarks. NOT: as platforms to build strategy for.

## 9. Historical Data and Ranking Context

### Current Ranking Problem (diagnosed per project brief)
The site has NOT been ranking for 6 months despite 180+ pages and 257 MDX articles. Potential causes this Source Context addresses:

1. **Brand migration signal disruption:** Rebrand from CalculateCreator to TT Calculator (March 2026) may have fractured entity identity in search engine knowledge bases. The old brand's historical data (positive or negative) is disconnected from the new domain.
2. **Possible negative historical data:** If pre-rebrand content generated poor engagement signals (high bounce, low time-on-page), those signals may carry forward even with new branding.
3. **Source Context inconsistency:** If older content was written with a different implicit Source Context (e.g., general social media tools vs TikTok-specific monetization), the site sends mixed entity identity signals.
4. **Content-to-component mismatch:** Calculator pages that lack functional calculator widgets, or guide pages that don't link to calculators, create misaligned search activity expectations.

### Source Context Remediation Priorities
- Ensure every page's title, H1, meta description, and body content reflect the Central Entity ("TikTok Creator Monetization") and Central Search Intent ("calculate, estimate, optimize")
- Verify site-wide n-grams appear consistently in boilerplate (header, footer, about page, methodology page)
- Audit for content that falls outside topical borders and either reframe it through the Source Context lens or remove it
- Confirm all calculator pages have functional interactive widgets (not just text)
- Strengthen entity identity by ensuring schema markup consistently references "TT Calculator" as the organization across all pages
