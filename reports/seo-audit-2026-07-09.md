## 1. Executive Summary

**The headline: this site is effectively invisible in organic search, and the reason is not the content — it's authority and trust.** Ahrefs shows **Domain Rating 0, 0 organic keywords, 0 organic traffic** across 300+ published pages. The on-site foundation is, in most respects, well-built (clean canonicals, valid schema, resolved thin-content, strong H1/alt hygiene). The binding constraints sit in three places:

1. **Authority is not just absent — it's toxic.** The backlink profile (314 referring domains) is dominated by spam/PBN link-selling domains and toxic anchor text that reads like **purchased Fiverr/PBN packages** bought right at launch. This is a link-scheme risk, not an asset.
2. **Trust/accuracy is undermined on the exact YMYL facts the site sells.** Content **hardcodes ~13 core money facts that contradict the site's own canonical data source** (`creator-data.ts`) and each other — including a **Critical false claim** that understates the current creator-pay program by 10–40×. For a site whose whole value proposition is "accurate earnings math," this is existential.
3. **A few single-root-cause bugs are leaking the little equity there is** — most visibly **252 broken "Open Calculator" money CTAs** and a site-wide footer that routes every page through redirects.

**Good news:** the technical and content foundation is strong enough that this is fixable with focused effort. Most of the highest-impact fixes are small, single-root-cause changes. But **no amount of on-page work will move rankings until the authority and trust problems are addressed** — those are the leading dominoes.

## 2. Critical Findings (fix first)

### C1 — Backlink profile is spam/PBN-dominated (likely purchased cheap-SEO packages)
The majority of the top referring domains are Ahrefs-flagged `is_spam: true` link-selling/PBN sites registered in clusters exactly as the site launched (first-seen March–June 2026): 10+ `itxoft-*-seo.site` siblings, 5 `fiverr-*-seo.site`, and `.agency`/`.shop` link farms (`buyseobacklinks.shop`, `pbnseolinks.shop`, `authoritybacklinks.shop`, `linkspro.agency`, `rankio.agency`…). There are essentially **zero genuine editorial backlinks** — the few non-spam refdomains are auto-generated stat-scraper sites (`siteprice.org`, `sitelike.org`).
**Fix:** Export the full refdomain list → build & submit a **Google disavow file** for the spam/PBN clusters → **stop buying any link packages** → if a manual action exists, file reconsideration after disavow. *(Detail: `08-offpage-authority.md` OFF-1.)*

### C2 — Toxic / spam anchor-text profile
The two largest anchors by referring-domain count are pure spam: 210 refdomains use *"Boost Rankings & Massive Traffic | High DR … Backlinks for Casino, Crypto …"* and 166 use an *"Enter SEOExpress.org!"* testimonial. Others literally say *"Fiverr backlinks boosted tiktokcalculator.net"* and *"thanks to linkspro.agency, tiktokcalculator.net achieved…"*. Legitimate branded anchors are a small minority.
**Fix:** Same disavow action as C1 removes most toxic anchors; going forward, earn descriptive branded/editorial anchors. *(Detail: OFF-2.)*

### C3 — False core pay claim understates the current program by 10–40× (YMYL trust)
Two live pages state *"The Creator Rewards Program (formerly the Creator Fund) pays between **$0.02 and $0.05** per 1,000 views"* — conflating the **legacy** Creator Fund rate with the **current** Creator Rewards program, which the site's own canonical data and other pages put at **$0.50–$2.00**.
- `content/data/earnings/average-tiktok-earnings-per-views.mdx:52`
- `content/blog/building-multiple-income-streams-tiktok.mdx:89`

**Fix (S):** Rewrite both to separate the two programs (Fund legacy $0.02–$0.05; Rewards current $0.50–$2.00). *(Detail: `02-query-eav-consistency.md` QE-7; this is the worst instance of the systemic hardcoding problem in §4.1.)*

### C4 — 252 broken "Open Calculator" CTAs (double-slash 404s)
The primary conversion CTA on blog/news/author pages points to double-slash URLs (`/calculators/tiktok-money//`) that 404. One template bug concatenates a path that already ends in `/`. Top offenders: `/calculators/tiktok-money//` (76×), `/calculators/niche-profitability//` (30×), `/calculators/engagement-rate//` (26×).
**Fix (S):** One CTA-template fix (collapse `//` → `/` / use `path.posix.join`) clears all 252. *(Detail: `06-internal-linking.md` IL-1.)*

## 3. High-Priority Findings by Theme

### 3.1 Authority & indexation reality (off-page)
- **No editorial links / zero organic footprint** (OFF-3, OFF-4). With DR 0 and 0 keywords, the site has nothing to rank on. **Verify indexation in Google Search Console immediately** — check Coverage (indexed vs excluded), Manual Actions, and Security Issues; run `site:tiktokcalculator.net`. *Note: the connected Ahrefs account has **no GSC project for this domain**, so first-party index/impression data is currently a blind spot (see §6 caveats).*

### 3.2 Content accuracy / E-E-A-T (the trust problem) — **root cause in §4.1**
Beyond the Critical C3, the site's money facts are internally contradictory across pages, contradicting the canonical `creator-data.ts`:
- **Follower-tier boundaries** contradict canonical on most pages (canonical mid 50K–200K/macro 200K–1M vs dominant content 100K–500K/500K–1M) — feeds *all* brand-deal and earnings math (QE-9).
- **Same 34,999-coin "Universe" gift → creator payout stated as $87.50 / $175 / $185.50 / $226 / $265** because pages mix two incompatible formulas (QE-6); the "Universe" coin cost itself is 44,999 vs 34,999 across pages (QE-5).
- **TikTok Shop commission** has no consistent standard (5% / 10% / 5-15% / 8-10%) and **two blog posts announce mutually exclusive "official" 2026 changes** — fabrication risk on YMYL (QE-11, QE-18).
- Coin USD value stated 5 ways; Creator Rewards RPM ranges $0.40–$2.05; diamond withdrawal minimum wrong on a live page (QE-4, QE-8, QE-12).

### 3.3 Internal linking & architecture
- **Site-wide footer routes every page through 3 redirects** (`/benchmarks/`, `/comparisons/`, `/reference/` → `/data/*`) = **2,121 redirect-hop links**. One shared-component edit fixes it (IL-2).
- **4 built pages are shadowed by their own redirect** (video-performance, watch-time, /tools/, a blog post) — dead HTML the code still links to (IL-3).
- **All 6 author pages are fully orphaned** — bylines link to `/about/` instead of `/authors/<name>/`, starving E-E-A-T author signals (IL-5). *(Compounds SF-4 and the `/authors/` hub 404 in TECH-2.)*

### 3.4 Semantic foundation
- **Glossary/definitional layer is unbuilt** — strategy calls for `/glossary/[term]/` nodes + calculator→glossary links; only a single hub exists, zero term pages (SF-3).
- **~26 of 43 calculator entities are attribute-orphans** (tool page only, no supporting articles), while 8 core entities carry 6–9 supporting pages — lopsided topical authority (SF-5).
- **Author/E-E-A-T outer section + 2 calculator hubs + 1 blog post are built but missing from the sitemap** (SF-4).

### 3.5 On-page & schema
- **43% of pages (151) use auto-generated templated meta descriptions** ending "…with data, benchmarks, and expert analysis" — scaled/duplicative CTR signal (OP-1).
- **52 calculator pages lack `og:image`**; generic `twitter:title` "TT Calculator" on 110 pages (OP-2, OP-4).
- **244 Article/NewsArticle entities lack a schema `image`** → no Article rich-result eligibility (SCH-1); the `@id` entity graph is disconnected (34/1,117 entities) and author `Person` entities lack `sameAs`/`image` (SCH-2, SCH-3).

### 3.6 Monetization / performance
- **Consent is a homegrown banner, not a Google-certified/IAB-TCF CMP** — ad-serving-suspension risk for EEA/UK/CH traffic (MON-2).
- **CSP doesn't whitelist AdSense runtime domains** — will throw CSP violations and suppress ad fill once slots are enabled (MON-1).
- **Google Fonts loaded via render-blocking CSS `@import`** (3 families, no preconnect, not self-hosted) — direct LCP/CLS hit (PERF-1).

## 4. Cross-Cutting Root Causes

### 4.1 Single-source-of-truth was never propagated to content
`creator-data.ts` is a clean, well-structured canonical data file — but MDX prose **hardcodes numbers** instead of rendering the constants. A 2026-03-18 "resolution" updated the constants file only; the ~350 content pages were never regenerated against it. **This one root cause drives ~13 of the factual contradictions** (QE-4 → QE-15, incl. Critical C3). *Durable fix:* template these values from the constant at render time where feasible, and/or run a content sweep + add a lint/test that greps content for divergent values.

### 4.2 Migration/remediation held on some axes but not the highest-frequency ones
The domain rename (calculatecreator → tiktokcalculator) and single-slash normalization **held** (0 wrong-domain, 0 missing-slash, clean canonicals). But the earlier "343 broken links" fix **did not hold** for the two most-repeated link classes: the 252 double-slash CTAs (C4) and the 2,121-link footer redirect hops (§3.3). Both are one-component fixes.

### 4.3 Topical map drifted from the live site (governance gap)
The site grew to ~2× the documented plan (352 vs "150+") under a re-organized taxonomy the map no longer reflects (SF-7, SF-8). Planned category hubs (`/comparisons`, `/reference`, `/niches`) are near-empty stubs while content moved into `/guides/*` and `/data/*`. The map can no longer be used to detect gaps or cannibalization — and there are **21 cannibalization clusters** where multiple URLs target one head query (QE-1).

### 4.4 Author-entity / E-E-A-T signals are systematically broken
Author pages are simultaneously: `noindex` (TECH-2), 404 at the `/authors/` hub (TECH-2), missing from the sitemap (SF-4), fully orphaned from internal links (IL-5), and lacking `sameAs`/`image` in schema (SCH-3) — with the visible author being the generic brand "TT Calculator" rather than a credentialed person (EEAT-1). For YMYL earnings content, this is a coordinated weakness worth fixing as one workstream.

## 8. Bottom Line

The site is a well-engineered content asset sitting on two cracked foundations: **no legitimate authority (and a toxic link profile that must be disavowed), and internally contradictory money facts that break trust on the exact YMYL topics it monetizes.** Fix those two, plus the handful of single-root-cause bugs leaking equity (broken CTAs, footer redirects, orphaned authors), and the strong technical/content base gives this site a real chance to earn its first organic visibility. **Priority order is authority + trust first, structure second, polish third — because on-page optimization cannot pay off until the site is trusted and can be crawled cleanly.**
