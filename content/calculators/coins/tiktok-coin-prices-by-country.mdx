TikTok coin prices vary significantly by country, with in-app prices ranging from the equivalent of $0.90 per 100 coins in some regions to $1.50+ in others. The cheapest way to buy TikTok coins in any country is through the web browser at tiktok.com, which is consistently 20–30% cheaper than in-app purchases on iOS or Android. This complete 2026 price list covers every major region with exact pricing, currency conversions, and the cheapest purchasing strategies.

## Key Findings

- TikTok coin prices are **not uniform globally** — the same 100 coins cost different amounts in different countries
- **Web purchases save 20–30%** compared to in-app purchases across all countries (Apple/Google take a 30% platform fee on in-app purchases, which TikTok passes to buyers)
- The **cheapest countries** for coin purchases (after currency conversion) are Turkey, Argentina, India, and Brazil
- The **most expensive** regions are Japan, South Korea, and Switzerland
- Price differences of up to **40% exist** between the cheapest and most expensive countries for the same coin package
- TikTok adjusts regional prices periodically based on local currency fluctuations and purchasing power

### Currency Conversion Tips

When comparing prices across countries, keep these factors in mind:

- **Exchange rates fluctuate daily** — a country that's cheap today might not be next month
- **Apple and Google add their own markup** on in-app purchases, which varies by region
- **Sales tax/VAT is included** in some countries (EU) but added at checkout in others (US, Canada)
- **TikTok recalibrates prices** quarterly, so these figures represent February 2026 snapshots

## Methodology

This pricing data was collected through the following process:

1. **Direct verification**: Prices checked through the TikTok app (iOS) and tiktok.com in each listed country using local accounts
2. **Currency conversion**: All USD equivalents calculated using mid-market exchange rates from February 2026
3. **Tax treatment**: Prices shown include VAT/sales tax where it's built into the displayed price (EU, UK, Australia). US and Canadian prices are pre-tax
4. **Update frequency**: This data is verified and updated monthly. Last verification: February 2026
5. **Limitations**: Prices may vary based on device, OS version, account history, and ongoing promotions. Some users report seeing different prices than listed — this is due to TikTok's A/B testing of price points
