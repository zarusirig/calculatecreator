### How to Buy TikTok Coins In-App (iOS and Android)

The in-app method is the most common way people purchase TikTok coins. It is straightforward but carries the highest per-coin cost because Apple and Google take a 30% cut of every transaction.

**Step 1: Open TikTok and go to your profile.** Tap the profile icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen.

**Step 2: Open Settings.** Tap the three-line menu icon in the top-right corner, then select "Settings and privacy."

**Step 3: Navigate to Balance.** Scroll down and tap "Balance." This screen shows your current coin total and the option to recharge (buy more coins).

**Step 4: Tap "Recharge."** You will see all available coin packages with their prices. Select the package you want.

**Step 5: Confirm payment.** TikTok processes the payment through Apple Pay, Google Pay, or your linked payment method. On iOS, you may need to authenticate with Face ID or your Apple ID password. On Android, Google Play handles the transaction.

**Step 6: Coins appear instantly.** Once payment clears, the coins are added to your wallet immediately. There is no waiting period.

The entire process takes under 60 seconds. The downside is cost: you are paying a premium of 20-30% compared to web pricing because of app store fees that TikTok passes on to the buyer.

### How to Buy TikTok Coins on the Web (Cheapest Method)

Buying through TikTok's website is the single best way to reduce your coin costs. This method bypasses app store fees entirely, and TikTok passes most of those savings along to the buyer.

**Step 1: Open a web browser on your computer or phone.** Navigate to [tiktok.com](https://www.tiktok.com) and log in to your account.

**Step 2: Click your profile icon.** In the top-right corner of the page, click your avatar to open the dropdown menu.

**Step 3: Select "Get Coins" or "Recharge."** TikTok's web interface presents coin packages at reduced prices compared to the in-app store. You will immediately notice the lower pricing.

**Step 4: Choose your package and payment method.** Web purchases support credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal in most regions. Select your preferred package and enter payment details.

**Step 5: Complete the purchase.** Confirm the transaction. Coins are credited to your account across all platforms — they will appear in your TikTok app on your phone within seconds.

The savings are substantial for regular buyers. A viewer who spends $100 per month on coins through the app would spend only $70-80 for the same number of coins through the web, saving $240-360 per year. ### Regional Pricing Differences

TikTok coin prices are not uniform worldwide. The same 100-coin bundle costs different amounts depending on your country, local currency, and applicable taxes. Buyers in some countries pay significantly less per coin than US-based buyers.

Key regional pricing factors include:

**App store price tiers.** Apple and Google set region-specific price tiers that do not perfectly track real-time exchange rates. A package priced at $0.69 in the US might cost the equivalent of $0.55 in India or $0.89 in Australia after currency conversion. These tiers are updated periodically, not continuously.

**VAT and digital goods taxes.** European Union countries add VAT (typically 19-25%) on top of the base coin price. Countries like Japan apply consumption tax. These taxes increase the effective cost per coin in those regions without changing the number of coins received.

**Purchasing power adjustments.** TikTok adjusts base pricing in select markets to reflect local economic conditions. Coins in India, Brazil, and parts of Southeast Asia are priced lower in absolute USD terms than in the United States, United Kingdom, or Australia. For a detailed comparison of pricing in every major market, see the full [TikTok coin prices by country](/calculators/coins/tiktok-coin-prices-by-country/) breakdown.

**Currency conversion timing.** If you purchase coins in a non-USD currency, the exchange rate is locked at the time of transaction by the payment processor. Buying when your local currency is strong against the dollar gives you better effective value.

### Discounts and Promotions

TikTok periodically offers coin discounts that can reduce costs beyond the standard web pricing advantage.

**Bonus coin events.** Several times per year, TikTok runs promotions where purchasing a coin package gives you bonus coins on top of the standard amount. A typical promotion might offer 10-20% bonus coins on packages of 1,321 coins or larger. These events are usually tied to holidays, platform milestones, or regional campaigns.

**First-time buyer offers.** New accounts occasionally see a discounted price on their first coin purchase, typically a small package at 30-50% off. This promotion is not guaranteed and varies by region.

**Creator-specific events.** During certain live events or battles, TikTok may offer temporary discounts on specific gifts or provide multiplied diamond payouts for creators. While these do not directly reduce your coin cost, they amplify the impact of your spending.

**Web-exclusive deals.** TikTok sometimes offers additional discounts on web purchases that are not available in-app. Check both the app and tiktok.com before making a large purchase to compare current pricing.

There is no reliable coupon code system for TikTok coins. Third-party websites that claim to offer TikTok coin discount codes are almost universally scams. Stick to official promotions within the TikTok app or website.

## Requirements

Before you can buy TikTok coins, your account must meet several conditions.

**Age requirement.** You must be at least 18 years old to purchase coins. TikTok enforces this through account age verification, and accounts registered with a birthdate under 18 will not see the coin purchase option. In some regions, the minimum age is higher due to local regulations.

**Valid payment method.** You need a credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or PayPal account linked to your TikTok account or app store account. Prepaid cards and gift cards work in some cases but are not guaranteed to process.

**Account in good standing.** Accounts that have been flagged for community guideline violations or that are under review may have coin purchasing disabled. If your account is temporarily restricted, you will need to resolve the issue before buying coins.

**Region availability.** TikTok coins are not available in every country. Some regions restrict virtual currency purchases due to local financial regulations. If you do not see the coin purchase option, your country may not currently support it.

**Internet connection.** Purchases require an active internet connection. Failed transactions due to connectivity issues can sometimes result in charges without coins being credited. If this happens, contact TikTok support with your transaction receipt.

## Common Issues and Troubleshooting

Even straightforward coin purchases can run into problems. Here are the most common issues and their solutions.

**"Recharge" button is missing.** This usually means your account age is set below 18, you are in a restricted region, or your account has a temporary hold. Check your birthdate in account settings. If the date is correct and you are over 18, contact TikTok support.

**Payment declined.** Your bank or card issuer may block the transaction as a suspected fraud attempt, especially for first-time purchases or large amounts. Call your bank to authorize the transaction, or try a different payment method. Switching to PayPal on the web often resolves card-specific blocks.

**Coins not appearing after purchase.** In rare cases, coins take a few minutes to credit after payment. Force-close and reopen the TikTok app. If coins still have not appeared after 30 minutes, check your email for a purchase receipt and contact TikTok support with the transaction ID.

**Double charges.** If you tapped the purchase button multiple times during a slow connection, you may see duplicate charges. TikTok typically auto-refunds duplicate transactions within 3-5 business days. If the refund does not appear, file a dispute through Apple, Google Play, or your payment provider.

**Web purchase not syncing to app.** Coins purchased on tiktok.com should appear on your mobile app immediately. If they do not, log out and back in on the app. Ensure you are logged into the same account on both web and mobile.

**Regional price mismatch.** If you are using a VPN, the coin prices shown may not match your payment method's country. TikTok prices coins based on your app store region or payment method location, not your IP address. Disconnect VPNs before purchasing to avoid pricing conflicts or failed transactions.

For persistent issues, TikTok's in-app support (Settings > Report a Problem) is the fastest path to resolution. Include screenshots of the error and any transaction receipts.
