The most expensive TikTok gift is the Universe at 34,999 coins ($370.99 at in-app prices), followed by the Lion at 29,999 coins ($317.99). The real dollar value that creators actually receive is roughly 50% of the gift's coin value — about $185.50 for a Universe and $159.00 for a Lion — because TikTok takes a commission when converting gifts to cash. This complete ranking covers every premium TikTok gift, what it costs the sender, and what the creator actually earns.

## Key Findings

- The **Universe** (34,999 coins) is the most expensive single gift, costing the sender $370.99 via in-app purchase or ~$367 via web
- Creators receive approximately **50% of a gift's value** after TikTok's diamond-to-cash conversion fee
- The top 10 most expensive gifts range from **5,000 to 34,999 coins** ($52.50 to $370.99)
- Buying coins through the web browser saves **23–29%**, making expensive gifts significantly cheaper for senders
- During TikTok Live battles, the **same gift generates more excitement** and can trigger gift cascades from other viewers
- Gift values have remained relatively stable since 2025, with TikTok occasionally adding new premium gifts

## Gift Value Chain — Extended Data

Understanding how TikTok gifts convert from coins to cash requires knowing the full value chain:

### The Coins → Diamonds → Cash Pipeline

| Step | What Happens | Value Retained |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Viewer buys coins | Viewer pays real money for TikTok coins | 100% |
| 2. Viewer sends gift | Coins convert to a gift animation | 100% (coins spent) |
| 3. Creator receives diamonds | Gift converts to diamonds at ~50% rate | ~50% |
| 4. Creator withdraws cash | Diamonds convert to cash | ~50% of original |

**The key conversion rate**: creators receive roughly 50% of the coin value. A gift worth 10,000 coins (about $106) earns the creator approximately $53, paid out as diamonds that cash out at $0.005 each.

This means TikTok effectively takes a **50% commission** on all gift transactions. The exact rate fluctuates slightly (some creators report 47–53%) depending on the gift type and withdrawal method.

## Methodology

This gift ranking data is compiled using the following approach:

1. **In-app verification**: All gift names, coin costs, and animations verified directly in the TikTok app (iOS, US account) as of February 2026
2. **Price calculation**: USD values calculated using the standard in-app rate of 100 coins = $1.29 and web rate of 100 coins = $0.99
3. **Creator payout estimation**: Based on the standard ~50% diamond-to-cash conversion ratio, verified with multiple creator earnings reports
4. **Gift availability**: Some gifts are seasonal or regional. This list covers gifts available to US accounts in February 2026
5. **Update frequency**: Gift catalog and pricing verified monthly. TikTok occasionally adds, removes, or reprices gifts without advance notice

Note: TikTok periodically introduces limited-time and event-specific gifts that may temporarily exceed the prices listed above. These are not included as they are not permanently available.
