## How TikTok LIVE Gifts Work



### 1. Viewers Buy Coins
              Purchase with real money through TikTok app. Prices: 100 coins = $0.99, 500 coins = $4.99, etc. TikTok keeps ~30% (standard app store cut).




### 2. Viewers Send Gifts (Costing Coins)
              Gifts range from 5 coins (Rose) to 44,999 coins (Universe). Gifts appear as animations during your LIVE. More expensive gifts = more impressive animations.




### 3. Creators Receive Diamonds
              Gifts convert to Diamonds (TikTok's creator currency). **Conversion rate: 1 Diamond ≈ $0.005 USD**. TikTok takes ~50% (viewer spends $1, creator receives $0.50 worth of diamonds).




### 4. Creators Cash Out
              Minimum withdrawal: $50 (10,000 diamonds). Payment via PayPal or bank transfer. Processing time: 7–10 days.

## TikTok LIVE Battles (Major Earnings Boost)
          **What Are LIVE Battles?** Two creators go LIVE simultaneously, viewers send gifts to "vote" for their favorite. Creator with most gifts (by value) wins.



### Earnings Boost:

              - Battles can earn 3–10× more than solo LIVE sessions
              - Competitive nature drives viewers to send high-value gifts
              - Both creators benefit (even "loser" earns more than solo stream)



            **Example:** Solo LIVE: $50 → LIVE Battle: $250–$500 (both creators combined)




### How to do LIVE battles:

              - Need 1,000+ followers (LIVE access)
              - Find creator at similar follower tier in your niche
              - During LIVE, tap "Battle" button → Invite creator or accept invite
              - 5-minute battle begins; viewers send gifts to support their creator
              - Both creators split earnings based on gifts received

            **Pro tip:** Schedule battles in advance, promote to both audiences → Higher turnout → Higher earnings.
