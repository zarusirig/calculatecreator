# How to Join TikTok Creator Fund

Step-by-step guide to qualifying, applying, and getting approved for TikTok's Creator Rewards Program (formerly the Creator Fund).

Last updated: March 2026 · 5 min read · 2026 Requirements

Joining the TikTok Creator Rewards Program is the first major monetization milestone for growing creators. Approved creators earn directly from eligible video views at a typical rate of **$0.02–$0.05 per 1,000 views**, creating passive income alongside brand deals and affiliate revenue. Qualifying requires **10,000 followers, 100,000 views in the last 30 days, videos over one minute long, an account at least 18 years of age, and residence in an eligible country**. This guide covers the exact application process, strategies to reach each threshold faster, common rejection reasons, and what to expect after approval.

## Quick Requirements Check

Confirm every requirement before applying — TikTok rejects applications missing any single threshold:

- **10,000+ followers**
- **100,000+ video views** in the last 30 days
- **Videos longer than 1 minute** (only 1-minute-plus videos monetize)
- **18+ years old**
- **Residence in an eligible country** (US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Brazil, and others)
- **Account in good standing** (no recent Community Guidelines violations)

**Pro tip:** Estimate your potential monthly earnings before applying with the [Creator Rewards calculator](/calculators/tiktok-creator-fund/).

## Step-by-Step Application Process

The application takes about 15 minutes once you meet every threshold.

1. **Verify you meet every eligibility requirement** — confirm 10K followers, 100K views in 30 days, 1-minute-plus videos, 18+ age, and an eligible country.
2. **Switch to a Creator or Business account** — open Settings → Manage Account → Switch to Creator Account. Creator accounts unlock analytics and monetization features.
3. **Open Creator tools** — navigate to Settings and Privacy → Creator tools → Creator Rewards Program. The option appears only when requirements are met.
4. **Review and accept the program terms** — read the Creator Rewards agreement, then accept the terms to submit your application.
5. **Wait for approval (1–3 business days)** — TikTok reviews applications within 1–3 business days and notifies you on approval. Rejected applicants reapply after 30 days.
6. **Set up payment information** — add PayPal or direct bank transfer in Creator tools → Creator Rewards → Payment settings. The minimum payout threshold is **$10**.

## How to Reach 10,000 Followers Faster

The follower threshold is the slowest requirement for most creators. Four tactics compress the timeline:

- **Lock one focused niche** — niche accounts grow **2–3x faster** than general pages.
- **Post 2–3 times daily** — daily posting roughly doubles follower growth versus weekly posting.
- **Hook viewers in the first second** — strong openings lift views **40–60%** and feed the For You feed.
- **Engage daily with your niche** — comment on 20–30 niche videos per day to drive profile visits and follows.

## How to Hit 100,000 Views in 30 Days

Views are cumulative across every video in the last 30 days, so posting volume compounds. Three paths reach 100K:

### Math Breakdown

- **Conservative:** 30 videos × 3,500 views each = **105,000 views**
- **Moderate:** 20 videos × 5,000 views each = **100,000 views**
- **One viral hit:** 1 video with 100K+ views, driven by a strong hook plus a trending format

### Strategies to Boost Views

- **Jump on trends immediately** — trending sounds lose effectiveness after **48 hours**.
- **Use niche hashtags** — #FinanceTok (27B+ views) outperforms #Finance (4B+ views) for discovery.
- **Build watch-time hooks** — phrases like "wait for it" raise completion rate and FYP reach.
- **Lead with shorter videos** — 7–15 second clips post higher completion rates that boost distribution.
- **Test posting times** — try 6 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM, and 9 PM to find your audience's peak.

## Common Rejection Reasons (And How to Fix Them)

Most rejections trace to one of five fixable issues:

| Rejection Reason | Fix |
| --- | --- |
| Under 10K followers or 100K views | Keep posting daily until both thresholds clear, then reapply after 30 days |
| Videos mostly under 1 minute | Shift to 1-minute-plus formats; only long videos monetize |
| Recent Community Guidelines violation | Serve the penalty period and keep the account clean before reapplying |
| Under 18 or ineligible region | Confirm age and country; the program is region-limited |
| Inactive account (30+ days, no posts) | Resume daily posting for 2–3 weeks before reapplying |

Reapply after 30 days once every issue is resolved.

## What Happens After You're Approved

Once approved, a Creator Rewards option appears in your Creator Tools dashboard. Eligible videos start earning immediately based on views and engagement.

### Your First 30 Days

- **Week 1:** No earnings yet — a 7-day processing delay applies.
- **Week 2:** First earnings appear, commonly **$2–$10** for small accounts.
- **Weeks 3–4:** Post daily, identify which formats earn most per view, and double down on them.
- **End of month 1:** You qualify for a first payout once earnings reach the **$10 minimum**.

### Payment Schedule

Earnings are calculated daily and paid monthly. Payments issue around the **20th–25th of each month** for the prior month's earnings (January earnings pay in late February). Payouts go to your chosen method: PayPal, Zelle, or direct deposit.

### Maintaining Your Status

TikTok removes creators from the program who violate Community Guidelines, drop below 10K followers, leave the account inactive for 30+ days, or engage in fraudulent activity. Keep the account in good standing to protect the income stream.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the requirements to join the TikTok Creator Fund?

The Creator Rewards Program (formerly the Creator Fund) requires **10,000 followers, 100,000 video views in the last 30 days, videos longer than 1 minute, age 18+, and residence in an eligible country**.

### How much does the TikTok Creator Fund pay per 1,000 views?

The program pays a typical **$0.02–$0.05 per 1,000 views**, varying by niche, audience geography, and engagement. Finance and tech niches commonly sit at the top of the range.

### How long does Creator Fund approval take?

TikTok reviews applications in **1–3 business days** and notifies you on approval. Rejected applicants reapply after 30 days.

### Why was my Creator Fund application rejected?

The most common reasons are missing the 10K-follower or 100K-view threshold, videos under 1 minute, a recent Community Guidelines violation, being under 18, or an inactive account. Resolve the issue and reapply after 30 days.

### What is the minimum payout for the TikTok Creator Fund?

The minimum payout threshold is **$10**. Earnings below $10 roll over until the balance clears the threshold, with monthly payments issued around the 20th–25th.
