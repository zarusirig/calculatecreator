## 1. TikTok Creator Rewards Program



### What Is the Creator Rewards Program?
            The Creator Rewards Program is TikTok's current in-app payout program. It pays eligible creators based on qualified views of original videos longer than one minute, and it replaced the legacy Creator Fund with rates roughly 10 to 40 times higher per view. Eligibility and the qualified-view rules are documented on TikTok's [official Creator Rewards Program page](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/creator-rewards-program). For a deep dive into [how Creator Rewards actually works](/guides/creator-rewards-program/), including RPM rates by niche and optimization strategies, check our complete guide.


              **Payment Rate:** $0.50–$2.00 per 1,000 qualified views (US baseline)

              **Example Earnings:** 500,000 monthly qualified views = $250–$1,000/month

              The legacy [Creator Fund](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/tiktok-creator-fund-us) paid only $0.02–$0.05 per 1,000 views (about $0.03 on average). It is not shut down everywhere, but Creator Rewards has superseded it in most markets.






### Eligibility Requirements

              - **10,000+ followers**
                  Must be authentic, engaged followers
              - **100,000+ video views in last 30 days**
                  Cumulative across all videos
              - **18+ years old**
                  Age verification required (19+ in South Korea)
              - **Original videos over 60 seconds**
                  Only original public videos at least one minute long earn rewards
              - **Account in good standing**
                  No community guideline violations

## 2. Brand Deals & Sponsorships

          Brand deals are the **highest-earning monetization stream** for most TikTok creators. Companies pay creators to feature their products or services in videos. Unlike the Creator Rewards Program's $0.50–$2.00 per 1,000 qualified views, brand deals can pay $0.05–$0.25 per follower. Our [complete brand deals guide](/guides/brand-deals/) covers everything from [finding your first sponsorships](/guides/how-to-get-brand-deals/) to [negotiating premium rates](/guides/how-to-negotiate-brand-deals/).




### Typical Brand Deal Rates


| Follower Tier | Rate Per Post | Monthly (2-4 deals) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Nano (1K-10K) | $50–$200 | $100–$800 |
| Micro (10K-50K) | $200–$1,000 | $400–$4,000 |
| Mid (50K-200K) | $1,000–$5,000 | $2,000–$20,000 |
| Macro (200K-1M) | $5,000–$15,000 | $10,000–$60,000 |
| Mega (1M+) | $15,000–$50,000 | $30,000–$200,000 |






### How to Get Brand Deals



#### 1. Join TikTok Creator Marketplace
                [TikTok Creator Marketplace](/guides/tiktok-creator-marketplace/) is the official platform connecting brands with creators. Requires 10K+ followers and strong engagement rate. Brands can discover and contact you directly.




#### 2. Create a Media Kit
                Professional PDF showcasing your stats, demographics, previous partnerships, and rates. Essential for pitching brands directly.




#### 3. Use Influencer Platforms
                Sign up for platforms like AspireIQ, CreatorIQ, or Hashtag Paid. They connect creators with brands actively seeking partnerships.




#### 4. Direct Outreach
                Email brands you love with a pitch. Works best for micro-creators (10K-50K) targeting smaller brands in your niche.

## 3. LIVE Gifts

          Once you reach 1,000 followers you can go LIVE, and at 18+ you can receive virtual gifts. Viewers buy coins (about $0.0106 each in-app), send gifts during your stream, and those gifts convert to diamonds you cash out. Creators keep approximately 50% of a gift's face value, so a viewer's $1.00 gift returns roughly $0.50 to you. TikTok's [official Video Gifts documentation](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/video-gifts-on-tiktok) covers diamonds and cash-out mechanics. Consistent streamers with an engaged community can earn a few hundred to several thousand dollars per month. Learn more in our [TikTok LIVE monetization guide](/guides/tiktok-live/).

## 4. TikTok Shop & Affiliate

          TikTok Shop lets you earn commission by tagging products in your videos and LIVE streams. Affiliate access opens at 5,000 followers, the standard commission is 5% (ranging 2%–20% by category), and TikTok charges a 2.9% platform fee per transaction with biweekly payouts. Because it needs no inventory, affiliate marketing is one of the fastest income streams to add once you qualify. Creators who demo products on LIVE convert 3–5x better than static video links. See our [TikTok Shop affiliate guide](/guides/tiktok-shop-affiliate/) to get started.




## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much money can you make on TikTok with 1 million followers?

With 1M followers, creators typically earn $5,000-$20,000/month. This breaks down to: the Creator Rewards Program ($200-$1,500), brand deals ($4,000-$15,000 for a few deals), and LIVE gifts ($500-$2,000). Top earners with high engagement can exceed $50,000/month through premium brand partnerships and their own products.

### How many followers do you need to make money on TikTok?

You can start earning with as few as 1,000 followers through nano-influencer brand deals ($50-$200/post). For the Creator Rewards Program, you need 10,000 followers. For TikTok LIVE, you need 1,000 followers. Most creators become profitable (earning $500+/month) around 25,000-50,000 followers.

### How much does TikTok pay per 1,000 views?

The current Creator Rewards Program pays $0.50-$2.00 per 1,000 qualified views in the US — roughly 10 to 40 times the legacy Creator Fund's $0.02-$0.05. That is still below YouTube's $2-$4 AdSense RPM, so most TikTok creators earn far more through brand deals, often the equivalent of several dollars per 1,000 views through sponsored content.

### What is the easiest way to make money on TikTok?

The easiest entry point is the Creator Rewards Program (passive income once you post qualifying 60-second videos). However, brand deals generate much more revenue with similar effort. For beginners with fewer than 10K followers, start by growing to Creator Rewards eligibility, then focus on pitching nano-influencer brand deals.

### Do you need 10,000 followers to make money on TikTok?

No. You can earn brand deals with 1,000+ followers, and TikTok LIVE gifts also unlock at 1,000 followers. At 10,000 followers you unlock the Creator Rewards Program, making platform payouts much easier. Most creators see consistent income ($500+/month) after hitting 25,000 followers with good engagement.
