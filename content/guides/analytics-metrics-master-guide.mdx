### FYP Distribution Factors


#### How TikTok Decides FYP Reach

                        - 1

TikTok creators:                             **Initial Test (200-500 viewers):** Posted video shown to small test audience. Algorithm measures completion rate, engagement within first hour.
                        - 2

                            **First Push (1K-10K viewers):** If test performs well (60%+ completion, 5%+ engagement), expanded to broader audience.
                        - 3

                            **Viral Threshold (100K+ viewers):** If performance remains strong (70%+ completion, shares increasing), pushed to massive FYP distribution.
                        - 4

                            **Sustained Distribution (1M+ viewers):** If metrics hold, video can stay on FYP for days/weeks. Shares and saves extend lifespan.

                  For creators selling products/services and brands running campaigns, business metrics track ROI and profitability.





### Conversion Rate Tracking
                      % of viewers who take desired action (click link, make purchase, sign up).


                        - **Good:** 1.5-3% for e-commerce
                        - **Excellent:** 5%+ conversion rate

### Customer Acquisition Cost
                      How much you spend to acquire one customer through TikTok.


                        - **Formula:** Total Spend / Customers Acquired
                        - **Good CAC:** &lt; 30% of Customer LTV
                        - Lower CAC = more profitable scaling


                        [Calculate CAC](/calculators/customer-acquisition-cost/)





### Lifetime Value (LTV)
                      Total revenue a customer generates over their relationship with you.


                        - **Formula:** Avg Purchase × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
                        - **Goal:** LTV should be 3-5x CAC
                        - Focus on retention to increase LTV


                        [Calculate LTV](/calculators/lifetime-value/)





### Campaign ROI
                      Return on investment for marketing campaigns.


                        - **Formula:** (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
                        - **Good ROI:** 150-300% for most campaigns
                        - Track all costs (ads, creator fees, production)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most important TikTok metric to track?

Completion rate is #1. It measures what percentage of viewers watch your video to the end. Videos with 70%+ completion rate get massive FYP distribution. It's more important than likes or views because it signals content quality to the algorithm. Use our Completion Rate Calculator to track this metric.

### What is a good engagement rate on TikTok?

5-10% is good, 10%+ is excellent. Most creators fall in 2-5% range. Smaller accounts (under 10K) typically have higher rates (8-15%). Engagement rate matters more than follower count for brand deals - 50K followers at 8% engagement is more valuable than 200K at 2%.

### How do I access TikTok Analytics?

Switch to Pro Account (Settings → Manage Account → Switch to Business/Creator Account). Analytics appear under Profile → Menu (3 lines) → Creator tools → Analytics. Requires 100+ followers. Data shows last 7/28/60 days of performance including views, followers, engagement, and audience demographics.

### What does completion rate mean on TikTok?

Completion rate = percentage of viewers who watched your entire video. Calculated as (Average Watch Time / Video Length) × 100. Example: 20-second video with 16-second average watch time = 80% completion. This is the strongest algorithmic signal - 80%+ completion often triggers viral distribution.

### How often should I check my TikTok analytics?

Weekly for strategic review, daily for active optimization. Check analytics every Monday to review last week's performance, identify top content, and plan upcoming week. If actively testing new strategies, check daily to quickly iterate. Monthly deep-dives for long-term trend analysis.

### What metrics do brands look for in TikTok creators?

Brands prioritize: (1) Engagement rate 5%+ minimum, (2) Audience demographics match target market, (3) Consistent niche/content theme, (4) Profile visit and follower conversion rates, (5) Average views per video. High engagement with relevant audience beats large follower count with low engagement.

### How can I improve my TikTok completion rate?

Hook viewers in first 3 seconds, keep videos 15-30 seconds for easier completion, use pattern interrupts every 5-7 seconds, eliminate dead air with fast cuts, end with loop back to beginning for replays, use trending audio, and deliver on your hook promise. Test different hooks on similar content to find what works.

### What is a good follower growth rate on TikTok?

1-2% daily growth is rapid, 0.5-1% is healthy, below 0.2% needs optimization. Growth rate = (New Followers / Starting Followers) / Days × 100. At 1% daily, you double every 70 days. Viral content can spike to 5-10% daily temporarily. Use Follower Growth Calculator to track and predict milestones.

### How does TikTok algorithm decide what goes viral?

Algorithm tests new videos on 200-500 viewers first. If completion rate hits 60%+ and engagement is strong, pushes to 1K-10K viewers. If metrics hold (70%+ completion, shares increasing), expands to 100K+ FYP distribution. Viral videos maintain strong metrics across multiple audience expansion waves. First hour performance is critical.

### Should I focus on views or engagement rate?

Both matter for different reasons. Views drive Creator Fund earnings and reach. Engagement rate drives brand deal value and long-term growth. Ideal: optimize for high engagement (quality audience) which naturally leads to higher views through algorithmic boost. 100K engaged followers beats 1M unengaged followers for monetization.
