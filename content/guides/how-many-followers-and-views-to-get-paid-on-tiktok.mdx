**You get paid on TikTok once you cross the follower and view thresholds for a specific earning program.** The first paid milestone is **1,000 followers**, which unlocks TikTok LIVE gifts and TikTok Shop affiliate links. The main pay-per-view program, the Creator Rewards Program, requires **10,000 followers plus 100,000 video views in the last 30 days**. Brand deals have no official follower minimum. There is no single "get paid" number on TikTok, because each income stream has its own gate.

Follower count and view count do different jobs. Followers unlock *access* to a program. Views inside that program determine *how much* you actually earn. The rest of this guide maps every threshold, explains the view rules that decide whether a video pays, and covers when the money lands in your account.

## How Many Followers Do You Need to Get Paid on TikTok?

You need **1,000 followers to start earning** through the first paid features, and **10,000 followers** to join the Creator Rewards Program, TikTok's primary view-based payout. Below 1,000 followers, your only realistic path to money is a brand deal, which carries no follower minimum but usually needs strong engagement (here is [how engagement rate is calculated](https://blog.hootsuite.com/calculate-engagement-rate/)) and a clear niche to attract.

Here is what unlocks at each follower tier. Treat regional rules as the deciding factor: availability, age minimums, and Shop requirements vary by country, so always confirm the current terms in [TikTok's official Help Center](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-and-business-accounts/understanding-your-analytics) and Creator Tools before you apply.

| Followers | What unlocks | Key extra requirements |
|---|---|---|
| 0+ | Brand deals and sponsorships | No official minimum; brands typically want 4%+ engagement and a defined niche |
| 1,000+ | Go LIVE, LIVE gifts, TikTok Shop affiliate | 18+ to go LIVE and to send or receive gifts; ID verification for Shop; region availability |
| 10,000+ | Creator Rewards Program (pay per qualified view) | 100,000 views in the last 30 days; 18+; original public videos over 1 minute; supported region |
| 100,000+ | TikTok Creator Marketplace (brand matchmaking) | Complete creator profile; portfolio; TikTok review (lowered in some regions) |

The pattern is clear: you do not need a huge audience to earn your first dollar. Most creators reach the 1,000-follower gate within a few months of consistent posting, start with LIVE gifts and Shop commissions, then layer on the Creator Rewards Program once they clear 10,000 followers.

## What Unlocks at Each Follower Tier

Each threshold represents a different type of income, and they stack rather than replace one another.

- **Brand deals (0+ followers).** There is no follower gate. Small "nano-influencer" deals go to accounts with a few hundred engaged followers in a specific niche. This is the fastest way to earn before you hit any platform threshold, because you negotiate directly with the brand.
- **LIVE gifts (1,000+ followers).** Once you reach 1,000 followers you can go LIVE, and viewers can send virtual gifts that convert into diamonds you cash out. You must be 18+ to send or receive gifts. See our [guide to qualifying for TikTok LIVE](/guides/how-to-qualify-for-tiktok-live/) for the full checklist.
- **TikTok Shop affiliate (1,000+ followers in most regions).** You earn a commission on products you promote through shoppable links. Requirements vary by country, but most regions expect around 1,000 followers, an account in good standing, and identity verification.
- **Creator Rewards Program (10,000+ followers).** This is TikTok's replacement for the old Creator Fund and the program most people mean by "getting paid for views." It requires 10,000 followers, 100,000 views in the last 30 days, and original public videos longer than one minute. Read the full [Creator Rewards Program breakdown](/guides/creator-rewards-program/) for the current rules.

For a complete side-by-side of every program's gates, see the [TikTok monetization requirements guide](/guides/tiktok-monetization-requirements/).

## How Many Views to Get Paid on TikTok

Inside the Creator Rewards Program, a video needs at least **1,000 qualified views** before it starts generating rewards. A qualified view is a genuine view from the For You feed in an eligible country; fake, bot, or ineligible traffic does not count toward the total. Views below that 1,000-qualified-view floor earn nothing from the program, even if the video is eligible in every other way.

Two separate view numbers matter, and creators often confuse them:

- **100,000 views in the last 30 days** is the *account-level* gate to join the Creator Rewards Program. It measures your channel's recent momentum.
- **1,000 qualified views per video** is the *video-level* floor to start earning on any single post once you are in the program.

Views convert to money through your RPM (revenue per 1,000 views), which for TikTok's view-based program is a planning figure commonly in the range of roughly $0.50 to $2.00 per 1,000 qualified views depending on niche, audience location, and content length. That is why creators rarely rely on views alone: a video with 1,000,000 qualified views might return roughly $500-$2,000 from the program itself, while a single brand deal can pay far more. Note that LIVE gifts and TikTok Shop affiliate commissions have **no view minimum** at all. LIVE gifts depend on viewers choosing to send gifts, and Shop commissions depend on sales, not views, so smaller accounts can earn from those streams well before they qualify for view-based pay.

## When Does TikTok Pay You?

TikTok pays you on a monthly cycle for view-based earnings and on a per-withdrawal basis for gifts and commerce. The timing depends on which program the money comes from.

- **Creator Rewards Program.** Earnings for a month are finalized on the 1st of the following month, and payments typically process around the 15th. The minimum payout is approximately $10. PayPal withdrawals usually clear in 24 to 48 hours once released.
- **LIVE gifts.** Gifts convert to diamonds immediately, but cashing out is a separate step. You request a withdrawal once you pass the minimum threshold (about $50, varies by region), and funds usually arrive within 5 to 10 business days.
- **TikTok Shop affiliate.** Commissions are released after the order is delivered and the return window closes, which often means 7 to 45 days after the order completes, depending on the product and region.
- **First withdrawals.** Your first payout from any program can take an extra 7 to 10 business days while TikTok verifies your identity, payment method, and tax documents.

A payment that has not arrived is usually still inside its normal window, not lost. If you want to plan cash flow around these cycles, the [TikTok payment delays guide](/guides/tiktok-payment-delays/) explains each timeline in detail.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many followers to make money on TikTok?

You can make money with zero followers through brand deals, but the first platform-based paid features unlock at 1,000 followers (LIVE gifts and TikTok Shop affiliate). The main view-based program, Creator Rewards, requires 10,000 followers plus 100,000 views in the last 30 days.

### How many views to get paid on TikTok?

Inside the Creator Rewards Program, each video needs at least 1,000 qualified views (real views from the For You feed in an eligible country) before it starts earning. Separately, your account needs 100,000 views in the last 30 days to qualify for the program in the first place.

### When does TikTok pay you?

For the Creator Rewards Program, TikTok finalizes each month's earnings on the 1st of the next month and processes payment around the 15th, once you pass the roughly $10 minimum. LIVE gift withdrawals arrive about 5 to 10 business days after you request them, and first-time payouts can take an extra 7 to 10 business days for verification.

### Can you get paid on TikTok without 10,000 followers?

Yes. Brand deals have no follower minimum, and LIVE gifts and TikTok Shop affiliate commissions unlock at 1,000 followers. Only the view-based Creator Rewards Program requires 10,000 followers, so many creators earn steadily from gifts, commissions, and sponsorships long before they qualify for it.

### Do views alone pay you on TikTok?

Only through the Creator Rewards Program, and only above the thresholds. Views under 1,000 qualified views per video earn nothing, and the RPM is moderate (a rough planning range of $0.50 to $2.00 per 1,000 qualified views), so most creators treat view-based pay as supplemental income rather than their main earnings.
