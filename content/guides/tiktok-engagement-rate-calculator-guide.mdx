## Section 3: TikTok Engagement Rate Formula


                  The engagement rate formula measures the percentage of viewers who interact with your content. TikTok calculates this metric differently than other platforms.




### TikTok Engagement Rate Formula


                        Engagement Rate = (Total Engagements ÷ Views) × 100

                      Standard engagement rate calculation





#### What Counts as Engagement?

                          - Likes
                          - Comments
                          - Shares
                          - Saves/Bookmarks
                          - Duets/Stitches
                          - Profile visits (sometimes)





#### What Doesn't Count?

                          - Auto-plays
                          - Watch time alone
                          - Profile views only
                          - Follows (usually)







### Important Notes

                      - Use **organic views only** (exclude purchased views)
                      - Calculate for **individual videos** or **account averages**
                      - Higher rates are better, but context matters (niche, content type)
                      - TikTok may show different engagement metrics than your calculations

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you calculate TikTok engagement rate?

**TikTok engagement rate = (Total engagements divided by Views) multiplied by 100.** Engagements include likes, comments, shares, and saves. Use organic views only. Exclude purchased views from the calculation.

### What

**A good engagement rate is 4 to 8%.** Excellent is 8%+, average is 2 to 4%, and below 2% needs improvement. Rates vary by niche and follower count.

### Why is my TikTok engagement rate low?

**Low engagement results from 5 common issues: poor content quality, wrong posting timing, lack of hooks, algorithm changes, or audience mismatch.** Use our calculator to track improvements over time.

### How often should I check my engagement rate?

**Check engagement rates weekly for individual videos and monthly for account averages.** Monitor trends rather than single video performance to identify patterns.

### Does TikTok show engagement rate in analytics?

**TikTok does not directly show engagement rate percentage.** It provides the raw metrics (views, likes, comments, and shares). Use our calculator to compute the rate from these numbers.
