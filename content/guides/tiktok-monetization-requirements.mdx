## How to Apply for Each Monetization Feature
          Once you meet the requirements, here is how to apply and get approved.





### Creator Fund Application Process

                - Open TikTok app and go to Profile
                - Tap the three lines (menu) in top right corner
                - Select "Creator Tools"
                - Tap "Creator Fund" (if eligible, you see the option)
                - Review requirements and tap "Apply"
                - Complete application form (personal details, tax information)
                - Submit government-issued ID for verification
                - Wait 3 to 7 days for review
                - If approved, earnings begin tracking immediately

              **Note:** Application is usually instant if you clearly meet requirements. Rejections are rare if follower/view counts are legitimate.





### LIVE Gifts Application Process

                - Ensure you have 1,000+ followers and are 18+
                - Go to Profile → Menu → Creator Tools
                - Select "LIVE Gifts"
                - Complete age verification (government ID required)
                - Accept the TikTok LIVE Gifts agreement
                - Approval is typically instant if requirements met
                - Go LIVE to start receiving gifts immediately

              **Tip:** Test your first LIVE during peak hours when your audience is active. Promote your LIVE 24 hours in advance with a post.





### TikTok Shop Affiliate Application

                - Meet regional requirements (varies by country)
                - Switch to Business Account if not already
                - Go to Profile → Menu → TikTok Shop
                - Select "Become an Affiliate"
                - Complete identity verification
                - Link payment method (PayPal, bank account)
                - Browse product catalog and start promoting
                - Create content with product links to earn commissions

              **Best Practice:** Only promote products you genuinely recommend. Authenticity drives conversions and maintains audience trust.





### Creator Marketplace Application

                - Reach 100,000+ followers (requirement lowered in some regions)
                - Visit creator.tiktok.com or apply through app
                - Complete comprehensive creator profile
                - Upload portfolio of your best content
                - Set your rates and preferred collaboration types
                - Wait for TikTok review (7 to 14 days)
                - After approval, brands discover and contact you directly

              **Note:** You can do brand deals before joining Creator Marketplace. The marketplace simply provides an official platform for connections.








## Smart Income Diversification Strategy
          Avoid relying on one income stream. The optimal income mix for a sustainable creator business follows this breakdown.






### Brand Deals & Sponsorships
                50 to 60%

              Primary income source. Highest earning potential per unit of effort. A single brand deal equals months of Creator Fund earnings.

              Example: 20K followers = $300 to $800 per sponsored post. Post 4 to 6 per month = $1,200 to $4,800 per month






### TikTok Shop Affiliate
                20 to 30%

              Growing income stream. Scalable with content volume. Earn 8 to 20% commission on product sales driven through your content.

              Example: $10,000 in product sales/month at 10% commission = $1,000/month passive income






### LIVE Gifts
                10 to 15%

              Community-driven income. Requires consistent LIVE streaming (2 to 4 times per week). Builds strong audience relationships.

              Example: 4 LIVE sessions per week, 100 viewers average, $150 to $400 per week = $600 to $1,600 per month






### Creator Fund
                5 to 10%

              Passive background income. Requires no extra effort beyond creating content. Lowest Return on Investment (ROI) but completely passive.

              Example: 3M views per month at $0.03 RPM = $90 per month (supplemental income only)
