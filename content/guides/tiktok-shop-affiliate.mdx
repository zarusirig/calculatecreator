## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much can I realistically earn as a TikTok Shop affiliate?

**Earnings vary widely by follower count, niche, and effort.** Creators with 10K to 50K followers typically earn $300 to $1,500 per month. Those with 50K to 100K followers earn $1,500 to $5,000 per month. Mega creators with 500K or more earn $20,000 or more per month. Beauty and fashion niches with high commission rates (8% to 20%) and engaged audiences perform best. Success requires consistent product promotion and authentic recommendations.

### What are the requirements to join TikTok Shop affiliate program?

**You need 1,000 or more followers (5,000 for US creators) and must be 18 years or older.** Your account must be in good standing with no violations. Post at least 1 video in the last 28 days. Accumulate at least 50 video views in the last 28 days. Reside in an eligible country (US, UK, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, or Vietnam).

### Which product categories have the best commission rates?

**Beauty and Skincare leads with 10% to 20% commissions.** Fashion and Accessories follows at 10% to 15%, then Health and Wellness at 8% to 15%. Electronics typically offer lower rates (2% to 8%) but have higher order values. Focus on categories with both good commission rates and products your audience needs to maximize conversions and earnings.

### How do I get products to promote as an affiliate?

**Browse the TikTok Shop product marketplace in your Creator Tools.** Request free samples from sellers, as many offer this to affiliates. Join the TikTok Shop Affiliate program to access product catalogs. Prioritize products with 4.5 star ratings or higher and good seller reviews. Test products before promoting to maintain audience trust and credibility.

### When and how do I get paid for TikTok Shop affiliate commissions?

**Commissions have a 7 to 15 day hold period after sale completion for potential returns or refunds.** After the hold period, earnings become available for withdrawal. Payments process weekly with a $10 minimum withdrawal. Withdraw via bank transfer or PayPal with 3 to 7 business day processing time. There are no withdrawal fees.




          Commission rates and program terms are subject to change. Always verify current rates in TikTok Shop Seller Center.
