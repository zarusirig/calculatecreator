TikTok Shop GMV surpassed $33 billion globally in 2025, marking a 115% increase from $15.4 billion in 2024 and establishing TikTok as the fastest-growing social commerce platform in the world. The integration of shopping directly into the TikTok content feed has created a new revenue stream for creators, with affiliate commissions and direct sales generating an estimated $3.2 billion in creator earnings. By 2028, TikTok Shop GMV is projected to exceed $80 billion as the platform expands into additional markets and deepens its product catalog.

## Key Findings

TikTok Shop's growth trajectory is unprecedented among social commerce platforms. Our analysis of quarterly GMV data, category performance, and geographic expansion reveals several defining trends.

The United States became TikTok Shop's largest single market in Q3 2025, surpassing Indonesia for the first time. US GMV reached an estimated $12.5 billion in 2025, up from $4.2 billion in 2024, fueled by aggressive seller onboarding, subsidized shipping programs, and the integration of Shop features into the For You Page algorithm.

Southeast Asia remains the largest regional market collectively, contributing approximately $14 billion in GMV across Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, and Malaysia. Indonesia alone accounts for roughly $8 billion, driven by the platform's early launch in 2021 and deep penetration into the Indonesian retail market.

Beauty and personal care is the dominant product category, accounting for 32% of total GMV. Fashion and apparel follows at 24%, with home and living at 14%. These three categories together represent 70% of all TikTok Shop transactions globally.

Creator-driven sales (through affiliate links and creator storefronts) account for approximately 45% of total GMV. The remaining 55% comes from brand-operated shops and paid advertising. This creator share has been declining slightly as more brands invest in their own TikTok Shop presence, but the absolute volume of creator-driven sales continues to grow rapidly.

The average order value on TikTok Shop is $28 globally, though this varies significantly by market — US average order value is $38, while Southeast Asian markets average $18 to $22. These relatively low order values reflect TikTok Shop's strength in impulse purchases and affordable consumer goods.

Beauty and personal care dominates because the product format translates perfectly to short-form video demonstrations. "Get ready with me" (GRWM) videos, product reviews, and transformation content create natural purchase intent that converts at rates 2-3x higher than static product listings on traditional e-commerce platforms.

Fashion and apparel is the fastest-growing major category, with 120% year-over-year growth driven by haul videos, outfit-of-the-day content, and the rise of affordable fast-fashion brands that have built their distribution strategy entirely around TikTok Shop.

Home and living has the highest growth rate (140% YoY) but from a smaller base. Viral "TikTok made me buy it" home products — from cleaning gadgets to kitchen tools — generate impulse purchases at scale. The creator affiliate share in this category is lower (35%) because many home brands operate their own TikTok Shop storefronts with paid advertising.

## Methodology

The GMV data in this article is compiled from multiple sources due to TikTok's limited public financial disclosures.

**Primary data sources** include TikTok's publicly announced GMV milestones (such as its $20 billion global GMV target disclosed in 2024), quarterly transaction volume estimates from third-party e-commerce analytics platforms (Marketplace Pulse, Sensor Tower, and data.ai), and aggregated seller revenue data from TikTok Shop seller communities and forums.

**Category and geographic breakdowns** are estimated using product listing data scraped from TikTok Shop's public catalog, category-level transaction data shared by analytics partners, and regional GMV contributions reported in trade press and investor presentations.

**Creator commission estimates** are derived by multiplying estimated creator-driven GMV (approximately 45% of total) by average commission rates (10-20%) reported by our survey panel of 850+ TikTok Shop affiliate creators.

**Projections** use a weighted model combining TikTok's announced expansion plans, historical quarter-over-quarter growth rates by region, consensus social commerce growth forecasts from eMarketer and Statista, and seller onboarding rate trends.

**Limitations.** ByteDance does not publicly report audited TikTok Shop financials. Our GMV estimates carry an uncertainty margin of approximately plus or minus 15%. Seasonal patterns may shift as the platform matures and expands into markets with different holiday shopping cycles. All figures are in USD, converted at average quarterly exchange rates.
