# Creator Fund vs LIVE Gifts: Which Pays More?

Last updated: July 2026 · 7 min read · Monetization Guide

TikTok creators have two main built-in ways to earn: **view-based payouts** on your videos and **LIVE streaming gifts** from real-time audiences. One important update before we compare them — the original **Creator Fund is discontinued**. [TikTok support](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-fund-us/creator-fund) states it is no longer available, and the view-based program that replaced it is the [Creator Rewards Program](/guides/creator-rewards-program/). Its rates are 10-40x higher than the legacy Fund, which changes this comparison substantially. This guide covers both the historical Fund numbers and today's Creator Rewards reality so you can decide which path to prioritize.

## Quick Comparison: Video Payouts vs LIVE Gifts

| Factor | Video Payouts (Creator Rewards) | LIVE Gifts |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Minimum Requirements | 10K followers, 100K views/30 days, 1+ min original videos | 1K followers, 18+ to receive gifts |
| Payment Rate | $0.50-$2.00 per 1K qualified views (estimate) | $0.005 per diamond (~50% of gift value) |
| Effort Type | Semi-passive (videos earn over time) | Active (must stream live) |
| Earning Potential | $250-$3,000+/month typical | $100-$10,000+/month possible |
| Payout Schedule | Roughly monthly after a validation window | Diamond withdrawals, typically 5-15 business days |

*For historical context, the legacy Creator Fund paid just $0.02-$0.05 per 1,000 views — roughly a tenth to a fortieth of Creator Rewards rates. Any comparison that still uses Fund numbers understates what view-based content pays today.*

## Video Payouts: Semi-Passive Earnings

### How view-based payouts work

TikTok pays creators for the qualified views their videos earn through an RPM (Revenue Per Mille) system. You earn as your videos accumulate qualified views over time, which makes this the closest thing TikTok offers to passive income. Under the [Creator Rewards Program](/guides/creator-rewards-program/), only original public videos longer than one minute qualify, and a video must reach 1,000 qualified For You feed views before it starts earning.

#### Requirements

- 10,000+ followers
- 100,000+ video views in the last 30 days
- 18+ years old (19 in South Korea)
- A Personal account in good standing
- Located in a supported region
- Original, public videos over one minute

### Typical view-based earnings

| Monthly Views | Legacy Creator Fund (historical) | Creator Rewards Estimate (if ~half qualify) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 100,000 | $2-$5 | $25-$100 |
| 1,000,000 | $20-$50 | $250-$1,000 |
| 10,000,000 | $200-$500 | $2,500-$10,000 |

The Creator Rewards column assumes roughly half of raw views qualify — only unique For You feed views watched longer than 5 seconds count. Both columns are planning estimates, since TikTok publishes no official rate card. Use our [Creator Fund Calculator](/calculators/tiktok-creator-fund/) to model your own view count.

## LIVE Gifts: Real-Time Audience Support

### How LIVE gifts work

During LIVE streams, viewers send virtual gifts bought with TikTok coins. Those gifts convert to diamonds, which you can withdraw as real money. TikTok takes roughly half of a gift's value, so a creator nets about $0.005 per diamond. Top streamers can earn thousands in a single session.

#### Requirements

- 1,000+ followers
- 16+ to go LIVE, 18+ to receive gifts
- Account in good standing
- Available in most countries

### LIVE gift value examples

| Gift Name | Coin Cost | Creator Receives |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rose | 1 coin ($0.01) | ~$0.005 |
| Hand Heart | 100 coins ($1.06) | ~$0.53 |
| Gold Mine | 1,000 coins ($10.60) | ~$5.30 |
| Drama Queen | 5,000 coins ($53.00) | ~$26.50 |
| Lion | 29,999 coins ($317.99) | ~$159.00 |
| TikTok Universe | 34,999 coins ($370.99) | ~$185.50 |

A viewer pays about $0.0106 per coin in-app (minimum purchase 65 coins for $0.69), and 2 coins convert to 1 diamond, so a creator keeps roughly half of what a viewer spends. Our [diamond and LIVE earnings calculator](/calculators/earnings-revenue/live-earnings/) converts gift totals into take-home pay.

### Typical LIVE earnings by streamer level

- **Beginner (1K-10K followers):** ~$5-$50 per stream, 10-50 average viewers
- **Intermediate (10K-100K followers):** ~$50-$500 per stream, 50-500 average viewers
- **Top streamer (100K+ followers):** ~$500-$5,000+ per stream, 500+ average viewers

## Pros and Cons

### Video payouts (Creator Rewards)

**Pros:** semi-passive income that keeps earning while you sleep; no real-time performance pressure; scales with your back catalog; rates are far higher than the old Fund.

**Cons:** requires 10K followers and 1+ minute original videos; short-form clips earn nothing; only qualified views count; earnings pass through a validation window before payout.

### LIVE gifts

**Pros:** much lower entry barrier (1K followers); high earning ceiling; direct audience connection; available in more countries.

**Cons:** requires active on-camera time; income is highly variable; TikTok keeps about 50% of gift value; not every niche suits live streaming.

## Real Earnings Comparison

### Small creator (10K-25K followers)

Roughly 500K monthly views:

- **Legacy Creator Fund:** ~$10-$25/month
- **Creator Rewards (est.):** ~$125-$500/month if about half of views qualify
- **LIVE Gifts:** ~$100-$400/month (4 streams/week at $25-$100/stream)

Under the old Fund, LIVE gifts won by 10-30x. Under Creator Rewards, video payouts are now genuinely competitive with LIVE at this tier.

### Medium creator (50K-100K followers)

Roughly 3M monthly views:

- **Legacy Creator Fund:** ~$60-$150/month
- **Creator Rewards (est.):** ~$750-$3,000/month if about half of views qualify
- **LIVE Gifts:** ~$800-$2,000/month (4 streams/week at $200-$500/stream)

The two are close enough that content style and available time — not raw rates — should drive the decision.

## When to Choose Each

**Prioritize video payouts when:** you prefer polished, edited videos over live content; you can consistently make original videos over one minute; you want semi-passive income without a streaming schedule; your niche has strong advertiser demand.

**Prioritize LIVE gifts when:** you are comfortable engaging with live audiences; you have a loyal, engaged community; you want a higher ceiling per hour of active work; you are under 10K followers and want to monetize now.

**Do both (recommended):** start LIVE streaming at 1,000 followers to earn immediately, grow to 10,000 to unlock Creator Rewards, then let your video catalog earn semi-passively while LIVE streams deepen community and lift views.

## Our Recommendation

Under the discontinued Creator Fund, LIVE gifts dominated at every follower tier. That verdict no longer holds: the [Creator Rewards Program](https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/creator-rewards-program) pays 10-40x more per view than the Fund did, so view-based income and LIVE gifts are now genuinely competitive. If you are comfortable on camera and have an engaged community, LIVE still offers the highest ceiling per hour. If you would rather build a library that earns over time, Creator Rewards is now a serious income stream in its own right. The most durable creators run both.

## FAQ

### Is the TikTok Creator Fund still available for this comparison?

No. The Creator Fund is discontinued, so the current view-based option is the Creator Rewards Program. The Fund's $0.02-$0.05 per 1,000 views rates appear here only as a historical benchmark.

### Do LIVE gifts pay more than Creator Rewards in 2026?

It depends on your tier and effort. LIVE gifts have a higher ceiling per active hour, but Creator Rewards now pays 10-40x more per view than the old Fund, making semi-passive video income competitive with LIVE at most follower levels.

### How much does a creator actually keep from a TikTok gift?

Roughly half the gift's value. Two coins convert to one diamond worth about $0.005 to the creator, so a 5,000-coin Drama Queen (about $53 to the viewer) nets the creator around $26.50.

### How many followers do you need for each?

LIVE gifts require 1,000 followers (and you must be 18+ to receive gifts). Creator Rewards requires 10,000 followers plus 100,000 views in the last 30 days and original videos over one minute.
